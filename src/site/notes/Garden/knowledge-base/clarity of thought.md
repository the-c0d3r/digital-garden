# [[Garden/knowledge-base/clarity of thought\|clarity of thought]]

Clarity of thought leads to clear actions, leading to clear consequences. 


1. When being asked to do something, try to understand what it is about, why somebody wants it done.
2. What might be the best way to do it?
3. put yourself in their shoes, how would they react?
4. ask yourself [[Garden/posts/So what\|So what]]?
